About Snappr

Snappr is a technology company headquartered in San Francisco, offering an on-demand professional photography booking service. Founded in 2016 by Matt Schiller and Ed Kearney, Snappr connects clients with pre-vetted photographers for various needs, from personal events to corporate shoots.

Our Services

  • On-demand photoshoots with as little as two hours' notice
  • AI-powered photo analysis tools for social media profiles
  • Automated visual content workflows for businesses
  • Access to a network of professional photographers worldwide
